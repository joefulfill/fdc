## üö® SPENDING RULES

1. **Delete food delivery apps** - Uber Eats, DoorDash, Favor gone from your phone
2. **Meal prep Sundays** - Follow DIET.md, batch cook for the week
3. **Coffee is a treat, not a habit** - Make coffee at home, limit shops to 2x/week
4. **24-hour rule on purchases over $50** - Sleep on it before buying
5. **One streaming service** - Pick Netflix, cancel the rest
6. **Cash envelope for discretionary** - Pull $200/week cash, when it's gone, stop spending
7. **No shopping when stressed** - Recognize emotional spending
8. **Review spending weekly** - 10 minutes every Sunday to track
